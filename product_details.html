ASUS ROG STRIX G15

15.6 inch, Intel Core i7-10750H, GTX 1650 Ti,8GB, 512GB SSD

The Strix G15 costs $999 and comes with an Intel Core i7-10750H processor, an Nvidia GeForce GTX 1650 Ti GPU with 4GB of VRAM, 8GB of RAM, a 512GB SSD and a 1080p, 144Hz display.
